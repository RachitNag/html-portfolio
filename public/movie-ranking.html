The Best Movies According To Rachit

My top 3 movies of all time.

1. Sam Bahadur

  • IMDB rating:7.7/10

"Sam Bahadur," released in 2023, is a biographical drama directed by Meghna Gulzar. The film chronicles the life of Field Marshal Sam Manekshaw, one of India's greatest military leaders. Vicky Kaushal stars in the titular role, portraying Manekshaw's valor and strategic brilliance during critical moments in Indian history. The movie delves into his personal and professional journey, highlighting his pivotal role in the 1971 Indo-Pak war. The narrative blends historical events with a compelling character study, celebrating Manekshaw's legacy and contributions to India's defense.

2. Sita Raman

  • IMDB rating:8.5/10

"Sita Ramam," released in 2022, is a romantic drama directed by Hanu Raghavapudi. The story revolves around Lieutenant Ram, played by Dulquer Salmaan, and his heartfelt love for Sita, portrayed by Mrunal Thakur. Set against the backdrop of the Indian Army in the 1960s, the film beautifully intertwines their love story with historical events. The narrative is rich with emotional depth, showcasing the power of love, sacrifice, and duty. With stunning visuals and a soulful soundtrack, "Sita Ramam" captures the essence of timeless romance.
